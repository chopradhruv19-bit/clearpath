## 📌 What is ClearPath?

ClearPath is an AI-powered tool that reads confusing government 
housing letters and instantly converts them into plain language — 
giving users a summary, urgency level, deadline, action checklist, 
and contact guidance in seconds.

No legal jargon. No confusion. Just clarity.

---

## 🎯 The Problem

- Government housing documents are written at a **Grade 16 reading level**
- Most housing notices give only **14 days** to respond
- **1 in 5** U.S. residents speak a language other than English at home
- Missing a deadline can mean **losing housing assistance entirely**

People miss out on help not because it doesn't exist — but because 
the information is too complex to act on.

---

## ✅ What ClearPath Does

Paste any government housing letter → ClearPath instantly returns:

1. **Plain English summary** — what the letter is actually saying
2. **Urgency level** — High / Medium / Low with reasoning
3. **Key deadline** — highlighted clearly so you never miss it
4. **Action checklist** — step by step what to do next
5. **Who to contact** — phone numbers and guidance pulled from the letter

---

## 🤖 How the AI Works

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Input | User pastes raw text of a government housing letter |
| AI Model | Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Anthropic) |
| AI Capabilities | NLP, Summarization, Classification |
| Output | Urgency badge, plain-English summary, deadline alert, checklist, contact info |

---

## 🛡️ Responsible AI

**Risk:** AI may misinterpret legal language and give incomplete advice

**Mitigation:** Every result displays a clear disclaimer — ClearPath 
explains letters in plain language but does not make legal decisions. 
Users are always advised to confirm with a housing advisor before acting.

**Human-in-the-Loop:** ClearPath never decides whether a user should 
comply, appeal, or ignore a notice. That decision always stays with a human.
